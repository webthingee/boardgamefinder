Privacy Policy for Board Game Finder Extension

Last Updated: [Current Date]

1. Data Collection
Board Game Finder does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not:
- Track your browsing history
- Store your searches
- Collect personal information
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Share any data with third parties

2. How the Extension Works
- The extension only reads text that you explicitly select when searching for board games
- Search results are displayed in new browser tabs
- All operations are performed locally in your browser
- No data is saved after the browser tabs are closed

3. Permissions
The extension requires certain permissions to function:
- activeTab: to read selected text when you choose to search
- scripting: to process your search requests
- tabs: to open search results
- contextMenus: to provide right-click search functionality
- tabGroups: to organize search results

4. Third-Party Websites
While this extension helps you search various board game websites, it does not control these websites. Please refer to each website's individual privacy policy when visiting them through our extension.

5. Updates to This Policy
If our data practices change, we will update this privacy policy accordingly.
